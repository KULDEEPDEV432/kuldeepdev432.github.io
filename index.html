How to Use

1. Download ReadyBoot.
2. Insert your USB drive.
3. Open ReadyBoot and select your ISO file.
4. Choose the USB drive from the list.
5. Click 'Create Bootable Drive'.
6. Wait for the process to finish.
7. Your bootable USB is ready to use!

Development

ReadyBoot is built using Python and Tkinter for the GUI. The app is developed for the Windows platform, with future plans to extend compatibility. Our goal is to offer a simple yet powerful tool that helps users without the need to rely on bulky third-party utilities.
